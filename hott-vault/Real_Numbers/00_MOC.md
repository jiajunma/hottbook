# 实数 / Real Numbers

## 概述 / Overview

两种构造：Dedekind 实数 (分割) 和 Cauchy 实数 (Cauchy 列的 HIT)。在可数选择下两者一致。Heine-Borel 紧性在构造性上成立。超实数可定义为 HIT。

Two constructions: Dedekind reals (cuts) and Cauchy reals (HIT of Cauchy sequences). With countable choice they agree. Heine-Borel compactness holds constructively. Surreal numbers can be defined as a HIT.
