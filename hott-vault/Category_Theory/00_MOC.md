# 范畴论 / Category Theory

## 概述 / Overview

在 HoTT 中，范畴是 $\mathsf{idtoiso}$ 为等价的预范畴 ("饱和"条件，类似于 univalence)。Rezk 完备化将任意预范畴普遍地嵌入范畴。结构恒等原理推广了 univalence。

In HoTT, a category is a precategory where $\mathsf{idtoiso}$ is an equivalence (the "saturation" condition, analogous to univalence). The Rezk completion universally embeds any precategory into a category. The structure identity principle generalizes univalence.
